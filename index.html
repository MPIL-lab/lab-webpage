The Multimodal Perception and Intelligence Learning Lab studies multimodal perception, computer vision, machine learning, and digital health. We develop AI methods for human behavior understanding, multimodal foundation models, and trustworthy intelligent systems, with the goal of building reliable models that can interpret complex visual and physiological signals in real-world settings.
Our lab is currently looking for motivated PhD Students and Visiting Students interested in multimodal AI, embodied intelligence, computer vision, and digital health. If interested, kindly contact us via email: j.liu81@lancaster.ac.uk.
