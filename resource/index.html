Datasets

  • UAV-Human Dataset

    CVPR 2021 benchmark for UAV-based human behavior understanding, with 67,428 multi-modal video sequences, 119 subjects, 155 action classes, and additional pose, ReID, and attribute annotations.

  • TrafficQA Dataset

    CVPR 2021 video question-answering benchmark for traffic-event reasoning, with 10,080 in-the-wild videos, 62,535 QA pairs, and 6 reasoning task types.

  • Animal Kingdom Dataset

    CVPR 2022 animal behavior understanding dataset, with 50 hours of annotated video, 30K action-recognition clips, 33K pose frames, and 850 species across 6 major animal classes.

  • Chaotic World Dataset

    ICCV 2023 chaotic-event human behavior benchmark, with dense annotations for spatiotemporal action localization, behavior-graph analysis, event grounding, and event sound-source localization.

  • Livestock (Cattle) Farm Dataset

    VCIP 2023 CattleEyeView top-down cattle dataset for counting, detection, pose estimation, tracking, and instance segmentation, with 753 cow instances in 30,703 frames across 14 video sequences.

  • NTU RGB+D Dataset

    CVPR 2016 large-scale RGB-D human action benchmark, with 56,880 action samples, 60 action classes, and RGB, depth, infrared, and 3D skeleton modalities.

  • SemTrack Dataset

    ECCV 2024 semantic tracking dataset in the wild, with 6.7M frames from 6,961 videos, 52 interaction classes, 115 object classes, 10 supercategories, and 12 scene types.
